Best Eats in Saipan

These are the top places to go for chow in Saipan!

Guangzhou Restaurant Himawari Oh My Grill! About Author

Guangzhou Restaurant

Guangzhou is a Chinese Restaurant located in front of Crowne Plaza, Garapan. Guangzhou is known for having delicious meals at affordable prices. They have an assortment of dishes popular in Chinese cuisine! You will surely be satisfied with any choice of meals!

Why I recommend:

  • Not Expensive: $4 Dollars a meal
  • Excellent Food and Service
  • Won 2nd Place For Best Chinese Restaurant

Himawari

Himawari is another spot I recommend because of the price and quality of food you will be receiving. Himawari is a very popular Japanese Convenient Store and Restaurant that has been serving Saipan for many years. They are best known for their homemade breads but also known for the restaurant!

Why I recommend:

  • Not Expensive: $8 Dollars a meal
  • Excellent Food and Service
  • Won 1st Place For Best Japanese Restaurant

Oh My Grill!

Barbeque lovers this restaurant is for you! Oh My Grill! is a very popular barbeque place that showcases the flavors of the CNMI! This place is the go to when I am craving barbeque and I never get disappointed. Definitely a go to spot when visting Saipan!

Why I recommend:

  • Not Expensive: $10 Dollars a meal
  • Excellent Food and Service
  • Won 1st Place For Best BBQ Restaurant
